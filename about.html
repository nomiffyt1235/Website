Welcome To Himalaya!

GROUP OF SCHOOL AND COLLEGES SYSTEM

Himalaya Degree College of Science and Technology (HCST) started its journey in 2013 under the capable and dynamic supervision of Mr. Sikandar Hayat. The objective behind this revolutionary step was to impart quality education especially in the field of Science and Technology, in this deprived area of KPK.

In initial step, HCST focused on Technical education and offered DAE Electrical, DAE Civil, short courses pertaining to technical jobs such as Survey, Material Testing, Land Survey, Quantity Survey, Electrical Supervisor, Optical Fiber Technician, Safety Officer, and Total Station etc. Setting new standards in the field of Technical Education, HCST embarked on its second journey. Besides Technical field, HCST offered F.Sc Pre-Medical, F.Sc Pre- Engineering, F.Sc Computer Science, Degree Programs B.Ed, M.Ed, M.Sc/BS HPE, ADE, DDM, JDPE, Diploma in computer science (DIT), Short courses in computer, office automation, AutoCAD etc. HCST is now planning to start BS Urdu, BS HPE, BS Education etc after the approval of the Sarhad University. Alhamdulillah, the dedication and success of HCST resulted in the birth of Himalaya School Dagai in 2020. It is a revolutionary step in the field of education in this far-flung area. The people of Dagai and surroundings are lucky to have a standard educational institution in their own native land. By the Grace of Allah and full dedication of Mr. Sikandar Hayat, Himalaya School has already set such standards that other institutions would take years to follow. The School offers Play group to F.Sc for boys and girls. HCST offers scholarships to talented students who demonstrate high standards of academic performance. Moreover, financial assistance is also extended to the needy students. The dedication on part of the management team, the honest and untired support of Mr. Sikandar Hayat accompanied with good luck and Grace of Allah, played a vital role in the flying success of HCST. HCST is committed to quality education and will strive hard to keep up with the modern world.
