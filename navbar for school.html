About Us

Himalaya Degree College of Science and Technology (HCST) started its journey in 2013 under the capable and dynamic supervision of Mr. Sikandar Hayat. The objective behind this revolutionary step was to impart quality education especially in the field of Science and Technology, in this deprived area of KPK. In initial step, HCST focused on Technical education and offered DAE Electrical, DAE Civil, short courses pertaining to technical jobs such as Survey, Material Testing, Land Survey, Quantity Survey, Electrical Supervisor, Optical Fiber Technician, Safety Officer, and Total Station etc. Setting new standards in the field of Technical Education, HCST embarked on its second journey. Besides Technical field, HCST offered F.Sc Pre-Medical, F.Sc Pre-Engineering, F.Sc Computer Science, Degree Programs B.Ed, M.Ed, M.Sc/BS HPE, ADE, DDM, JDPE, Diploma in computer science (DIT), Short courses in computer, office automation, autocad etc. HCST is now planning to start BS Urdu, BS HPE, BS Education etc after the approval of the Sarhad University.
