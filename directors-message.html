Director's Message

Since its own inception, man is engaged in a tussle to improve his life style and environment. The role of technology for this purpose hardly needs to be emphasized. To make students’ future secure and to make them the citizens of future, HCST Swabi would prove as a milestone in the field of quality education. At HCST Swabi we are committed to impart quality education to the budding technologist of Pakistan. In this era of globalization, only those nations can have an upper hand which can make best use of technology.

“THE GREAT AIM OF EDUCATION IS NOT KNOWLEDGE BUT ACTION”

At HCST Swabi we are committed to impart quality education to the budding technologist of Pakistan. In this era of globalization, only those nations can have an upper hand which can make best use of technology.


It gives me an immense pleasure to introduce Himalaya College of Science and Technology Swabi. Since the inception of this unique institution, our aim is to provide quality Technical and science education and develop competent individuals, who are technically and socially sensitive and committed to excellence at global arena. In accordance with this aim, we have a team of professional and experienced faculty members delivering academic and non academic contribution for achieving excellence in teaching theory and practical as well as research. The HCST Swabi has a glorious standing now and continues to evolve as the most reputed educational institution in the field of Science and Technology. Our programs / policies are designed in such a way to impart knowledge, leadership, innovative spirit, team work and global perspective to the students. The kind of exposure provided here not just train students for technological superiority but also make them ready to face global challenges.
As managing director, I am extremely proud of my team, dedicated to take on challenges. I am honored to lead my dedicated team which is not just about making something happen, but making something better. This is an exciting endeavor as we continue to enhance our programs to maintain national eminence.
Finally I would like to thank all the students, guardians/ parents , faculty members, staff, local residents and local government administration who have contributed a lot for smooth running of the system, I also thank all the board members for their continuous guidance and full support. Good Luck!

Mr. Sikandar Hayat Director
